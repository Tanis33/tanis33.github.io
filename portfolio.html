LGWI Water Monitor

As part of my senior project at Calvin University, I developed the LGWI Water Monitor to assist Life Giving Water International in monitoring water usage in rural Ecuador. The app uses computer vision to scan and log water meter readings, calculate household charges, and store data in Firebase. My focus was on front-end development, making the app user-friendly for local operators. This project empowers communities with better access to clean water management.
